# Narrative Modules — Festive Advent Math Journey

Each section below equips the Story Weaver output for one Colab notebook. Use the beats, explanations, and gossip snippets to keep a 13-year-old explorer charmed while reinforcing the math blueprint. References blend accessible videos/articles plus textbooks for stretch goals.

## Day 1 — Exploring Infinity: Countable vs. Uncountable Sets
- **Story beats:** Santa panics because the Nice List now spans galaxies; the elves try to number every name; Cantor the Candy Cane Sage shows why some wishes escape counting.
- **Friendly explanation:** Walk through finite vs. infinite sets, then rehearse enumerating rationals before unleashing the diagonal argument in approachable prose.
- **Gossip snippet:** Rumor says Comet the reindeer tried to index snowflakes and retired after realizing real numbers refuse to stand in line.
- **Reference pointers:** 3Blue1Brown “Cantor’s Infinity Hotel” video; Chapter on infinite sets from Velleman’s *How to Prove It*.
- **Call to action:** Explain, in your own story voice, why the set of real numbers cannot be listed like Santa’s gifts.
